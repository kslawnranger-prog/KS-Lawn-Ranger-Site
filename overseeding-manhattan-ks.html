Overseeding FAQ

When should I overseed in Kansas?

Fall is best (September–October). Cooler temps and fall moisture help seed establish before winter and surge in spring.

Do I need aeration before overseeding?

It’s strongly recommended. Aeration improves seed-to-soil contact and gives new grass a better chance to take.

How long until I can mow?

Typically once the new grass is tall enough—often around 3–4 inches. We’ll give you a simple timeline based on weather.
