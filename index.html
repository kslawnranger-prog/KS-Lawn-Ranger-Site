Frequently Asked Questions

How much does lawn mowing cost?

Pricing depends on lot size, obstacles, and trimming. Most residential visits are quoted after a quick on‑site check—text your address for a fast estimate.

When should I aerate and overseed in Kansas?

Fall is ideal (September–October). Cool temps and fall moisture help new roots establish before winter and surge in spring.

Do you haul away debris?

Yes—we remove leaves, sticks, branches, and lawn waste as part of our cleanup services.

What areas do you service?

Manhattan, Wamego, St. George, Alma, and Junction City. See the map below for a visual.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, check, Venmo, and QuickBooks Online payment link. Ask if you need invoices for records—we use QuickBooks for all estimates and billing.

Are you insured?

Yes. We’re fully insured and can provide proof on request.
